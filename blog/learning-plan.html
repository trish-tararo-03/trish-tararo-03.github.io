30 June 2022

Learning Plan

My commitment plan and accountability towards my learning journey with Dev Academy

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

I have always wanted a career that would allow me to be location free. A career where I could work with others to create something useful, or an experience that could change lives. A path that strays from the un-conventional. A career that gives back, one that my family can benefit from for generations. One that breaks the cycle. This is where Web Development comes into play, because it ticks all of those boxes. At the moment, I am enjoying the front-end side of Web development so I would like to progress in this area, perhaps learning the UX/UI design side also so I could be a design driven developer.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

I learn best by diving in and trying out different things to see what works. I am comfortable with receiving feedback and constructive critique. I am comfortable with failing. I can pick up on concepts quickly and apply them to a problem. Sometimes this approach can waste time as a lack of planning, or direction can result in time not spent productively, or time wasted obsessing over small details or going down rabbit holes on topics.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I want to build a consistant habit to improve myself. To improve on my learning ability. To improve my communication skills with others. To be the one to reach out first. To build appreciation and grattitude for the little things, the big things. All the things. To crave that hunger, that drive that can propel my life in a new direction. As a legendary queen said: “If you can’t love yourself, how in the hell are you gonna love anybody else? Can I get an amen?!”

An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives.

I think, for myself to succeed, I may almost have to gamify my life. I need to make two checklists daily. One list should include the minimum amount of work or tasks that I would need to complete in order to not create stress for my future self. The other list should contain the minimum amount, plus the tasks that, if I were in peak condition, could also complete. This way, I will have a daily goal to complete. During foundations, scheduling time to work on your sprints, and really put in the work. I think that to work safely with others in this particular learning environment is to express thoughts and opinions freely and allow others that same courtesy to do the same.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you?

Realize that breaks are necessary for your brain to process information. Music is your best friend and a great way to change your mood. Go see friends who are not afraid to tell me the truth and get a reality check. A glass of wine and a charcuterie board is always a great time.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

If it is a technical problem that I cannot solve using google, make use of the discord channel. I have found great success with using this avenue so far. We are lucky to have great facilitators who are always around to offer support. I have a good support system in my mum and other key family members that always bring me back to earth for mental and emotional support. I also have dear friends who respect me and challenge my way of thinking.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team.

I just want to know and feel that their support is there when I need it most. To have a channel of communication between facilitator and student, where I can feel safe and express concerns, worries without judgement. To get constructive feedback about growth.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things. For example, you might have whānau commitments like school drop-offs. It's important to plan these in.

I am a caregiver for my mother, so any appointments relating to her health is of high importance to me. I have taken this into consideration in relation to bootcamp.
